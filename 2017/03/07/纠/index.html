诗和远方の纠

Posted on 2017-03-07 | | Visitors

I recently began to study Arabic, French, and Italian. Apart from learning languages, I started to thinking a lot of things, rather than future, but the state that I want to be. I began to pick up the dreams and feelings that I desired when I was very young. I feel confused, passion, and desperate. The reason of it is because I met a Professor, I will call him T.

T is a professor, but I didn’t know it, and I even send emails with title Dear Dr. To be a good teacher like him, I know he will not care about this. I know him for a very long time, like 2 or 3 years, I’ve been hearing this name, but I never know who is he and even didn’t have the interest to take look at the photo on the website. Until this semester, I have a class of him.

He is a real gentlemen, he will do everything in a soft and gentle manner, but clear he is smart, what is important is that he has a creative mind. I can feel that he is passionated about the domain that he is working on. Somehow I was attracted by him during the class. He is the best teacher I’ve never seen before. He really know how to intriguing the students, and catch the attention of them. I could say to be on his class is very enjoyable. For example, he would keep asking questions, and careful and patiently explain the answer, more importantly is that he will give us a very interesting question at the end of a lesson. Of course I was the most active student in the class since T is such a attractive person.

At the beginning, I noticing that he has a very special accent, and later I found out that I really love that accent ! Sexy is not that accurate, but it could be described like this. His teeth is very special, because he has a 虎牙 at the same position like me. And he always has smiling face. His eyes are so beautiful. I like it when he looked at me and smiling, I will keep looking at him until he move his gazing eyes to other place.

I began crazy about him, so I searched his information as many as possible. It was amazing ! He could speak Arabic, Italian, and English. He really love art photography. 他浑身充满着我的一种向往。

I still remember that he told me that there is no lab and it turns out to be actually it has a lab. Although I was late. He found me at the last and apologized to me.

“Hey, wow you are so lovely today :)(with his pretty smile), uhmm, Do you remember that I told you in the class?” ”

“Oh, You mean the printer?

“No, it is not about the printer, It is about that the day I told you there is no lab this week, because I found that the lab was booked, and later it turns out the room was not booked.”

“Oh I see, so I am late”

然后他用一种我经常会用的表情看着我。疑惑且觉得神奇的表情。这种表情我在今天问他语言的时候，我说我无法发出意大利语的大舌音，纠结了好久，他说没关系的，我们听得懂，我说真的吗，他说：Of course !，然后也同样露出了这个表情。

他是那样的绅士。
